THE PROGRAM

MAGICIAN!

Our tremendous show leaves you in Madam Maggie's hands!
She will know how to sulk at you literally, how to remove you and more still!
Don't be frightened, her magic has nothing malefic!
Only your happiness will make Madam Maggie's enjoyment!

CLOWN!

In the second part, Charli the clown will make you twist to laugh!
Juggling, jokes, booby-trapped flowers, balloons of all the forms and all the colors!
Charli will know how to amuse you! Attention on the watering can!

TAMER!

In the third part of our international show, come to discover
our tamer Moogli and his tiger Sherkan!
Unprotected, Moogli will surprise you by showing you the big friendship
which there is between these two old friends!
Various jumps, ring of fire and more still wait for you under our big top!

CONTORTIONISTS!

Simone, Paulette and Ginette, every three comings of Asia.
They will surprise you in their show of contortion.
A soft melody rests you, whereas three women expose you their know-how.
In a grace without comparison, we would even believe it is very easy to execute.
But that's not the case!

MARIANE!

In the 5th part you will meet Mariane.
Raised in the forest, she is in accordance with the nature.
You will discover the agreement that it has with all the animals.
The children will be surprised! An eagle gives a kiss to a horse!

DROMEDARY!

Come to Camel's meeting! The sixth part will make you travel!
But Camel is a camel or a dromedary?
How does he made to balance on a thread?
How does he manage to go into this so small box?
You will be amazed by this show coming from so far!

TRAPEZE ARTIST!

She flies! It is in any case the impression that she will leave with you!
Suspended without net from 40 meters high, she will make surprising moves!
A sensation of freedom, a matchless grace,
Swallow cleaves through the air silently.
So raise the head towards stars and open big eyes!
